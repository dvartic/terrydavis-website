Terry A. Davis Tribute Page

Terry A. Davis was a programmer who is mostly known for developing the TempleOS operating system. This was an incredible untertaking for just one person: he developed everything from scratch, including its original programming language, editor, compiler and kernel.

Unfortunately Terry suffered from schizophrenia, a condition that severely impacted his life. His story has inspired many and helped to create awareness on the challenges faced by those living with schizophrenia.

I created this little website to honor him and his legacy. Rest in peace Terry!
